Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our ingredients, delivery, and subscriptions.

Where do your ingredients come from?

We grow 80% of our fruits in our own organic orchards. The remaining 20% is sourced from trusted local farmers who strictly follow zero-pesticide practices.

Are there vegan options available?

Absolutely! Over 90% of our menu is fully vegan. Any dressings or toppings that contain dairy or honey are clearly marked on our menu.

How fast is the delivery?

We guarantee delivery within 30 minutes for addresses within our primary service zones. We prepare your food the moment your order is confirmed.

Can I customize my own salad bowl?

Yes, you can! Use our mobile app to use the 'Build Your Bowl' feature, allowing you to choose your base, up to 5 fruits, and your favorite healthy dressing.
